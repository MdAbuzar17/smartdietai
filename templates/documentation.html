System Overview

SmartDiet AI is an intelligent diet and nutrition tracking application that combines artificial intelligence, machine learning, and nutritional science to help users achieve their health and fitness goals.

Key Features

1. AI-Powered Food Detection

  • YOLO v8 Object Detection: Upload a photo of your meal and let AI identify food items
  • Automatic Calorie Estimation: Estimates portion sizes and calculates calories
  • 35+ Indian Foods: Specialized model trained on Indian cuisine
  • Visual Feedback: See bounding boxes and nutrition info on your image

2. Manual Food Tracking

  • Search & Add: Search for any food item with portion sizes
  • Meal Categories: Track Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks, and Dinner separately
  • Real-time Macros: See protein, carbs, and fat progress with visual indicators
  • Daily Summaries: Pie charts showing your progress towards daily goals

3. ML-Based Diet Recommendations

  • Personalized Meal Plans: Get AI-generated meal suggestions
  • Dietary Preferences: Respects vegan/vegetarian choices
  • Allergy Safety: Filters out allergens automatically
  • Meal Time Appropriate: Different suggestions for breakfast, lunch, and dinner

4. Progress Analytics

  • Weight Tracking: Daily and weekly weight charts
  • Detailed Reports: View nutrition breakdown for any day or week
  • Trend Analysis: Visualize your journey over time
  • Calorie Deficit Calculation: See how you're progressing towards goals

Getting Started

Step 1: Registration

  1. Click "Register" on the home page
  2. Enter your email and create a strong password (must include uppercase, lowercase, digit, and special character)
  3. Submit the registration form

Step 2: Profile Setup

  1. After login, you'll be directed to profile setup
  2. Enter your personal details:
    • Age, gender, weight, height
    • Activity level (sedentary to extra active)
    • Dietary preferences (vegan/non-vegan)
    • Any food allergies
  3. The system automatically calculates:
    • BMI: Body Mass Index
    • BMR: Basal Metabolic Rate
    • Daily Calories: Based on your activity level
    • Macros: Protein, carbs, and fat targets

AI Food Detection

How It Works

  1. Navigate to "Detect Food" from the home page
  2. Upload an image of your meal (JPG, JPEG, or PNG)
  3. The YOLO model detects all food items in the image
  4. Each detected item shows:
    • Food name
    • Estimated portion size (grams)
    • Calories
    • Macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat)
  5. Total calories for the entire meal are calculated
ðŸ’¡ Tip: For best results, take photos with the food clearly visible. A plate in the image helps with portion size estimation!

Supported Foods

Food Tracking

Adding Food Manually

  1. Go to "Tracking" page
  2. Click "Add Food" for the desired meal time
  3. Enter:
    • Food name
    • Portion size (number)
    • Unit (grams, cup, piece, small/medium/large)
  4. The system fetches nutrition data from Spoonacular API
  5. Food is added to your daily log

Viewing Progress

The tracking page shows:

  • Meal Lists: All foods you've eaten for each meal
  • Progress Circles: Visual indicators for calories, protein, carbs, and fat
  • Percentage Complete: How close you are to daily goals
  • Remove Option: Delete foods if entered incorrectly

Diet Recommendations

Getting Recommendations

  2. The ML model analyzes:

  3. Receive personalized suggestions for:
    • Breakfast
    • Lunch
    • Snacks
    • Dinner

Recommendation Features

  • Nutritional Balance: Each recommendation includes full nutrition info
  • Variety: Different foods for each meal time
  • Safety Checks: Automatically filters out allergens
  • Preference Aware: Respects vegan/vegetarian choices

Progress Monitoring

Daily Progress

Technology Stack

Backend

  • Framework: Flask (Python)
  • Database: SQLite3
  • APIs: Spoonacular (nutrition data)

AI/ML Components

  • Object Detection: YOLO v8 (Ultralytics)
  • Recommendations: XGBoost/Decision Tree
  • Image Processing: OpenCV
  • ML Libraries: scikit-learn, pandas, numpy

Frontend

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • Bootstrap 4: Responsive design
  • Chart.js: Progress visualization
  • Font Awesome: Icons
